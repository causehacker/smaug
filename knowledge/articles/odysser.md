An AI-powered platform that transforms talking-head and marketing videos into engaging, animated content with motion graphics. Built on Framer, Odysser makes video production faster, more branded, and easier for creators.

## Key Features

- Convert talking-head videos into animated content
- AI-powered motion graphics generation
- On-brand customization for consistent visual identity
- Fast rendering and quick turnaround times
- Easy-to-use interface for creators of all skill levels
- Support for marketing videos and promotional content

## Use Cases

- Marketing and promotional video production
- Corporate video enhancement
- Social media video content creation
- Product demo videos with dynamic animation
- Educational video production
