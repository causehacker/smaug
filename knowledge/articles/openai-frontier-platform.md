# OpenAI Frontier Platform

## Overview
OpenAI Frontier is a new enterprise platform designed to help organizations build, deploy, and manage AI coworkers that can perform real work.

## Key Capabilities
According to OpenAI, Frontier agents have the following capabilities:

- **Understand how work gets done** - Agents comprehend workflow and business processes
- **Use a computer and tools** - Agents can interact with computers and various tools
- **Improve quality over time** - Continuous learning and refinement of agent performance
- **Stay governed & observable** - Built-in governance and monitoring capabilities for enterprise compliance

## Purpose
Frontier enables enterprises to create AI coworkers that operate autonomously while maintaining proper oversight and governance controls.
