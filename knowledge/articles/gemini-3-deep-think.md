Google has released a major upgrade to Gemini 3 Deep Think, their specialized reasoning mode designed to drive practical applications for researchers and engineers. The update enables complex tasks like interpreting scientific data, modeling physical systems through code, and turning sketches into 3D-printable objects.

## Key Features

- Analyzes drawings and builds complex 3D shapes
- Generates files for 3D printing from sketches
- Built for scientific and engineering applications
- Enables researchers to interpret complex data
- Allows engineers to model physical systems through code

## Availability

Rolling out to Google AI Ultra subscribers via the "Deep Think" option in the tools menu.
